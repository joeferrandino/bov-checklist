Broker Opinion of Value

Two Ways to Value Your Property

Choose the level of analysis that fits your timeline and goals.
Option A
Comprehensive BOV
The most accurate and strategic valuation available.
What I'll Need From You
  • Current rent roll and full copies of leases (or detailed lease abstracts)
  • Trailing 12-month operating expense statement (P&L)
  • Any pending lease negotiations, renewals, or known tenant issues
  • Major capital improvements completed in the last 5 years (roof, HVAC, paving, etc.)
The Benefits
  • Stronger strategic guidance on hold vs. sell, timing, and pricing
  • Most accurate, defensible valuation
  • Granular lease analysis
  • True NOI based on actual recoveries vs. expenses
  • Helps uncover and unlock hidden value
  • Surfaces potential risks buyers will flag
  • Underwriting that holds up to buyer scrutiny
Timeline Typically 5–9 business days, depending on full submission of documents.
Option B
Express BOV
A quick value estimate based on high-level information.
What I'll Need From You
  • Tenant list: name of tenant, square footage of space, monthly rent, and when their lease started and ends
  • Vacancy and renewals: note any vacant spaces, any tenants on month-to-month, and any you know are planning to leave or stay
  • Property expenses: annual costs for real estate tax, property insurance, electric, heating fuel, water & sewer, trash removal, repairs & maintenance, landscaping, and snow removal
  • Other recurring costs: property management, payroll, contract services, professional and legal, general and administrative, and leasing and advertising
  • Tenant Paid vs Owner Paid: note which utilities and expenses are paid by the tenant, paid by you, or shared between you
  • Major capital improvements completed in the last 5 years (roof, HVAC, paving, etc.)
The Benefits
  • Requires only basic information to get started
  • Gives you a ballpark value for early decisions
  • Useful for testing the waters before a full process
  • Helpful for internal planning or initial conversations
Timeline Typically 2–4 business days.
